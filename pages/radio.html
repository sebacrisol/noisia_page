With Noisia Radio we built a platform where we played a wider range of music than we did in a club environment. It allowed us to give some insights on the music we were feeling and releasing, as well as keeping our fans up to date more frequently
and in a more accessible way than just by playing a show near you once a year or via the occasional video or message on social media.
